How Voice Modeling Works

  • When you record your voice, we create a unique voice model
  • This model captures your vocal characteristics
  • When you select your custom voice, we apply this model to any text
  • The system generates speech that sounds like you speaking
  • Your voice model is stored locally in your browser
  • Note: Processing may take a few seconds after recording
